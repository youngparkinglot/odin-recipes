Chili

Classic Homemade Chili

This delicious homemade chili will leave your eyes watering! Stay tuned!

Ingredients

  • Aromatics
  • Ground Beef
  • Beans
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chili Seasoning
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Dice onion and mince garlic.
  2. Add ground beef.
  3. Drain beans and add to pot.
  4. Place a lid on pot and let simmer
  5. Taste!

Carbonara

Ultimate Spaghetti Carbonara

Discover how to make traditional spaghetti carbonara.

Hold your breath if you're hungry!

Ingredients

  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 2 plump garlic cloves, peeled and left whole
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil
  2. Finely chop the pancetta, having first removed any rind. Finely grate 50g pecorino cheese and 50g parmesan and mix them together
  3. Beat the eggs in a medium bowl and season with black pepper
  4. add 1 tsp of salt to the water, and then the spaghetti when the water comes back to a boil
